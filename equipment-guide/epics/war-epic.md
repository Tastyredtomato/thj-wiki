## Jagged Blade of War: Quest Guide
### (Blade of Tactics & Strategy)
<br><br>
<div class="wiki-content">
  <h2>Part 1 – Red Scabbard</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Head to East Freeport and find <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/10117">Kargek Redblade.</a> Say “what favor” to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20673">Wax Sealed Note.</a></li>
    <li>Head to Feerrott, give <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/47126">Oknoggin Stonesmacker</a> your <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20673">Wax Sealed Note</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20674">Tiny Lute.</a></li>
    <li>Head back to East Freeport, give <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/10117">Kargek Redblade</a> your <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20674">Tiny Lute</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/18083">Redblade's Legacy.</a></li>
    <li>Head to Permafrost Keep, kill a <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/73038">goblin wizard.</a> Loot a <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/10549">Heart of Frost.</a></li>
    <li>OPTIONAL: While you’re here, you can grab a <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20665">Block of Permafrost</a> from the ice giants or Lady Vox. If you choose to do the optional sidequest you'll need it.</li>
    <li>Head to East Karana, give <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/15077">Tenal Redblade</a> your <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/18083">Redblade's Legacy.</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20680">Totem of the Freezing War.</a>
      <ul>
        <li>Tenal Redblade can be found on the far east side of the zone at the bottom of the gorge below the Highpass Hold zoneline.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Give <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/15077">Tenal Redblade</a> both the <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20680">Totem of the Freezing War.</a> and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/10549">Heart of Frost.</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20681">Totem of Fiery War.</a>
    <ul>At this point the only items you should have are <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20681">Totem of Fiery War</a> and the Optional:<a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20665">Block of Permafrost.</a></ul>
    <li>Next, collect 4 items from raid bosses:
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/11582">Green Dragon Scales</a> – Drops from:
          <ul>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/94009">Severilous</a> – Emerald Jungle</li>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/108043">Hoshkar</a> – Veeshan’s Peak</li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/11622">Red Dragon Scales</a> – Drops from:
          <ul>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/32040">Lord Nagafen</a> – Nagafen’s Lair</li>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/120126">Melalafen</a> – The Western Wastes</li>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/91093">Talendor</a> – Skyfire Mountains</li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20676">Hand of the Maestro</a> – Drops in Plane of Hate from <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/186025">Hand of the Maestro.</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20679">Spiroc Wingblade</a> – Drops in Plane of Sky on Island 5 from <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/71012">The Spiroc Lord.</a> If you leave The Spiroc Guardian up (Island 5 boss), The Spiroc Lord will respawn upon death, allowing multiple attempts. It does not always drop; use this method if The Spiroc Lord is not visibly holding it.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Once all four items are obtained, head back to East Karana to <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/15077">Tenal Redblade</a>.
      <ul>
        <li>First, give him: <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/11582">Green Dragon Scales</a>, <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/11622">Red Dragon Scales</a>, <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20681">Totem of Fiery War</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20683">Mark of the Sword.</a></li>
        <li>Then, give him: <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20683">Mark of the Sword</a> and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20676">Hand of the Maestro</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20684">Tenal’s note to Kargek.</a></li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Now go back to East Freeport and find <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/10117">Kargek Redblade.</a> Give him <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20679">Spiroc Wingblade</a> and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20684">Tenal’s note to Kargek</a> to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/17859">Red Scabbard.</a></li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Part 2 – Ancient Sword Blade (2 options)</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Option 1:</strong>
      <ul>
        <li>Head to the Ocean of Tears and kill <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/409135">Denken Strongpick</a> and loot <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20667">Anicent Sword Blade.</a> There is no faction hit or negatives for doing this other than your guilty conscience for killing a defenseless dwarf.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li><strong>Option 2:</strong>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>This entire portion is optional and not required if you did Option 1:</strong>
        <li>Have a Tinkerer make you 2x <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/16889">Rebreathers</a> (Crafted, Tinkering, 175 Trivial – Tradable; check the Bazaar):
          <ul>
            <li>Rebreather:
              <ul>
                <li>1x Aqualung</li>
                <li>1x Firewater</li>
                <li>1x Lime Coasted Meshing</li>
                <li>1x Metal Fastening</li>
                <li>1x Platemail Helm</li>
                <li>1x Sharkskin Tubing</li>
              </ul>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li>Aqualung (Crafted, Tinkering 148 Trivial):
          <ul>
            <li>Fine Coral Mesh – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
            <li>Gnomish Bolts – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
            <li>Metal Rod – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
            <li>Metal Twine – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
            <li>Silk Lined Steel Helm – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
            <li>Fresh Fish – Bought (Klen Ironstove – PoK, -80, +237, Eastern Trader Building)</li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li>Firewater (Crafted, Tinkering, 17 Trivial):
          <ul>
            <li>Gnomish Spirits – Bought (Klen Ironstove – PoK, -80, +237, Eastern Trader Building)</li>
            <li>Water Flask – Bought (Klen Ironstove – PoK, -80, +237, Eastern Trader Building)</li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li>Lime Coasted Meshing – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
        <li>Metal Fastening – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
        <li>Platemail Helm – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
        <li>Sharkskin Tubing – Bought (Tabben Bronal – PoK, -387, +540, Southeastern Trader Building)</li>
        <li>Collect <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20665">Block of Permafrost</a> and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20664">Keg of Vox Tail Ale</a>:
          <ul>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20665">Block of Permafrost</a> – Drops from Ice Giants in Permafrost Keep (should already be obtained if following the guide).</li>
            <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20664">Keg of Vox Tail Ale</a>:
              <ul>
                <li>Good Races – Bought (Maula Fishcatcher – Commonlands; Loc: +510, +2390)</li>
                <li>Evil Races – Bought (Mariz Ixtaz – Neriak; Loc: -163, -938)</li>
              </ul>
            </li>
          </ul>
        </li>
        <li>Go to Ocean of Tears and locate Denken Strongpick.</li>
        <li>Give him 2x <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/16889">Rebreathers</a>, <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20665">Block of Permafrost</a>, and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20664">Keg of Vox Tail Ale</a> to receive the <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20667">Anicent Sword Blade.</a> Which is the exact same item from Option 1.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Part 3 – Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Collect 4 Items:
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20669">Severely Damaged Dragon Head Hilt</a> – Not Available in Echoes: Ground spawn in Timorous Deep (beneath the pieces at the chessboard).</li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20678">Giant Sized Monocle</a> – Drops from <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/86144">a mountain giant patriarch</a> in Dreadlands.</li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20666">Rejesiam Ore</a> – Give your <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20678">Giant Sized Monocle</a> to <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/92148">Mentrax Mountainbone</a> in Frontier Mountains (found in the back of the cave within the giant fort) to receive<a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20666">Rejesiam Ore.</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20677">Ball of Everliving Golem</a> – Drops from <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/72000">Dread</a>, <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/72004">Fright</a>, and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/72002">Terror</a> in Plane of Fear.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Next, go to <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/10116">Wenden Blackhammer</a> in East Freeport and give him:
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20669">Severely Damaged Dragon Head Hilt</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20666">Rejesiam Ore</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20677">Ball of Everliving Golem</a></li>
      </ul>
      to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20672">Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt.</a>
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Part 4 – Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Collect 4 items:
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20668">Unjeweled Dragon Head Hilt</a> – Not Available in Echoes: Ground spawn in Lake Rathetear (look for a greenish white flame; the Hilt is almost directly below it on the lake floor. Loc: +2512, +924, -198)</li>
        <li>Diamond – Random world drop gem.</li>
        <li>Black Sapphire – Random world drop gem.</li>
        <li>Jacinth – Random world drop gem.</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Next, go to <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/10116">Wenden Blackhammer</a> in East Freeport and give him:
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20668">Unjeweled Dragon Head Hilt</a></li>
        <li>Diamond</li>
        <li>Black Sapphire</li>
        <li>Jacinth</li>
      </ul>
      to receive <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20671">Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt.</a>
    </li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Part 5 – Ancient Blade</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Head to Chardok and kill <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/npc/103055">Queen Velazul Di`zok</a> and loot <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20670">Ancient Blade.</a></li>
  </ul>

  <h2>Part 6 – Final Turn-in: Jagged Blade of War</h2>
  <ul>
    <li>Combine your quest rewards from sections Part 2-5 into your <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/17859">Red Scabbard.</a></li>
    <li>Items to combine:
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20667">Anicent Sword Blade.</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20672">Finely Crafted Dragon Head Hilt.</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20671">Jeweled Dragon Head Hilt.</a></li>
        <li><a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/20670">Ancient Blade.</a></li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>You can turn the <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2010908">Jagged Blade of War</a> into <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2010910">Blade of Strategy</a> and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2066176">Blade of Tactics</a> by combining it by itself in the <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/17859">Red Scabbard.</a></li>
    <li>You can get your <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2010908">Jagged Blade of War</a> back by combining <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2010910">Blade of Strategy</a> and <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2066176">Blade of Tactics</a> in the <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/17859">Red Scabbard.</a></li>
  </ul>
