<h1>New to THJ Tips!</h1>

 

<p>Welcome to a quick rundown of must-know tips for The Heroes’ Journey server (THJ). This guide covers <strong>sympathetic items</strong>, <strong>skill leveling</strong>, <strong>bag farming</strong>, <strong>Bazaar NPCs</strong>, and a short <strong>FAQ</strong> on faction and item leveling.</p>

 

<hr>

 

<h2>1) Sympathetic Items – Big Boosts to Damage & Healing</h2>

<p>Sympathetic effects provide significant bonuses to both <strong>damage</strong> and <strong>healing</strong>. Here are some highlights:</p>

 

<ul>

 <li><strong>Tutorial Ring:</strong> Gains <em>Minor Sympathetic Strike</em> once it becomes <strong>Legendary</strong>.</li>

 <li><strong>Pal/Clr Summoned Hammers:</strong> Come with <em>Sympathetic Healing I</em>.</li>

</ul>

 

<h3>Farmable Sympathetic Items (1–50)</h3>

<ul>

 <li><strong>Sacrificial Dagger (Strike II):</strong><br>

   - Dropped in Mistmoore from a demon near the GY/crypt area (PH involved).<br>

   - <em>Alternative:</em> Mithril Quill in SolA’s gnome castle area.

 </li>

 <li><strong>Fine Steel Warhammer (Heal II):</strong><br>

   - Ideal if dropped as Legendary, but any version is still worth watching for.

 </li>

 <li><strong>Black Tome with Silver Runes (Strike III):</strong><br>

   - Dropped by Frog Tactician in LGuk (live side, King room).<br>

   - Likely the easiest Strike III item in Classic.

 </li>

 <li><strong>Prayer Shawl (Healing III):</strong><br>

   - Supposedly drops from Estrella in Kedge. (<em>Unconfirmed</em>).

 </li>

</ul>

 

<p><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> Use <em>RetributionEQ Alla</em> to search for items and effects. On the Ret server, “Apocryphal” = “Legendary.” Items must be <strong>Apoc/Legendary</strong> to gain special effects like Sympathetic Strike.</p>

 

<p>For this server, you can use a fan-made site:

 <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc" target="_blank">THJ DI</a>.

 <br>Example item link:

 <a href="https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2013400" target="_blank">https://www.thjdi.cc/item/2013400</a>

</p>

 

<hr>

 

<h2>2) Skill Leveling + Sympathetic Items Synergy</h2>

<p>To maximize synergy between skill leveling and sympathetic items:</p>

<ol>

 <li><strong>Spam low-level spells (Lvl 1-ish)</strong> as you go:

   <ul>

     <li>Adds extra damage.</li>

     <li>Keeps your spell skills and channeling high.</li>

   </ul>

 </li>

 <li><strong>Equip sympathetic items</strong> to benefit from extra damage/healing procs <em>and</em> keep skills up.</li>

</ol>

 

<hr>

 

<h2>3) Bags – A Must-Have for Capacity & Weight Reduction</h2>

<p>Bags <strong>cannot</strong> be upgraded in the power slot, but <strong>Enchanted/Legendary</strong> bags often have:</p>

<ul>

 <li>Increased capacity</li>

 <li>Weight reduction (WR)</li>

</ul>

 

<h3>LGuk Farming Options</h3>

<ul>

 <li><strong>Supplier Bags:</strong> not lore; farm multiple.</li>

 <li><strong>Evil Eye Bags:</strong> also not lore; grab as many as you can.</li>

</ul>

 

<p><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> If you’re leveling in LGuk (any side), kill these mobs regularly to pick up extra bags.</p>

 

<h3>Bazaar Vendor Option</h3>

<p>There’s a bag vendor in the Bazaar selling <strong>super cheap 20-slot large bank boxes</strong>. Stock up for more storage!</p>

 

<hr>

 

<h2>4) Bazaar NPCs of Note</h2>

 

<h3>Spell Vendors/Trainers</h3>

<ul>

 <li>Located on the <strong>north side</strong> of the Bazaar.</li>

 <li>Offer spells up to <strong>Level 50</strong> (with a few exceptions).</li>

 <li><strong>Tip:</strong> Buy spells in bulk for multiple level ranges (10–25, 25–40, etc.).</li>

</ul>

 

<h3>Tearel (Teleport NPC)</h3>

<ul>

 <li>Hail Tearel, choose a destination, then <em>click the map</em> to teleport.</li>

 <li><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> Look for <strong>glowing stones</strong> while exploring to unlock more teleport options.</li>

</ul>

 

<h3>Vision of Ayonae (Class Reset NPC)</h3>

<ul>

 <li>Found on the <strong>east side</strong> of the Bazaar.</li>

 <li>Allows you to remove one class to add another.</li>

 <li><strong>Cooldown:</strong> 15–30 days (TBD after launch).</li>

 <li><strong>Cost:</strong> Requires <em>Essence of Might (EoM)</em>.</li>

</ul>

 

<h3>SW Bank NPCs</h3>

<ul>

 <li><strong>Parcels NPC:</strong> for mail/parcel service.</li>

 <li><strong>Fading Memory Quest NPC:</strong> for a specific quest line.</li>

</ul>

 

<p><strong>Bonus Tip:</strong> Check out the center of the Bazaar for more merchants and items!</p>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

 

<h3>How does Starting Faction work?</h3>

<ul>

 <li>Based on <strong>class, race, and deity</strong>.</li>

 <li>Affects guard KOS status and epic quest progression.</li>

 <li><strong>Recommendation:</strong> Choose Agnostic if possible.</li>

 <li>If you plan a specific trio of characters, pick your race first; go Agnostic if available.</li>

</ul>

 

<h3>How does Item Leveling work?</h3>

<ol>

 <li>Only items in the <strong>Power Slot</strong> receive XP.</li>

 <li><strong>Bags and Augs</strong> do not level.</li>

 <li>If you can’t equip it, you can’t level it.</li>

 <li>Once an item gains XP, it becomes <strong>NO TRADE</strong>.</li>

 <li>Item leveling does not reduce your personal XP gain.</li>

 <li><strong>Leveling speed:</strong>

   <ul>

     <li>More stats = slower leveling.</li>

     <li>Sympathetic items take even longer.</li>

     <li>Approx. 4x Regular = 1x Enchanted, 4x Enchanted = 1x Legendary.</li>

   </ul>

 </li>

 <li>Use the <em>“Consume Item”</em> AA to handle extra items — it’s essential!</li>

</ol>

 

<h3>Lore/No Drop on Items?</h3>

<ul>

 <li>Many items normally flagged <strong>No Drop</strong> or <strong>Lore</strong> are <strong>tradeable</strong> here.</li>

 <li>Includes many <strong>epic quest</strong> drops — good for pre-Kunark farming.</li>

 <li><strong>Pro Tip:</strong> Sell or give away Phinny drops you don’t need!</li>

 <li>Equipping normal/enchanted gear <strong>doesn’t attune</strong> it unless:

   <ul>

     <li>You <em>use a clicky</em> on it, or</li>

     <li>You <em>kill a monster</em> with it in the <strong>Power Slot</strong>.</li>

   </ul>

 </li>

</ul>

 

<h3>Options for Faction Issues/Turn-ins?</h3>

<ul>

 <li><strong>Sneak</strong> (without Hide) behind an NPC to become indifferent.</li>

 <li><strong>Feign Death (FD)</strong> can help with turn-ins similarly.</li>

 <li><strong>Legendary Masks</strong> (e.g., <em>Mask of Deception</em> from LGuk assassin) become All/All, useful for:

   <ul>

     <li>Fixing faction problems.</li>

     <li>Bypassing locked doors.</li>

   </ul>

 </li>

</ul>
