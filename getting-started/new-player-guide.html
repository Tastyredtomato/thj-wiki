Character Creation & Logging In

After creating your character and logging in, you will find yourself in the Bazaar on Luclin.

Steps to Get Started:

  1. Check your cursor for an item given at character creation.
  2. Customize your User Interface (UI) to suit your preferences.
  3. Prepare to select your additional classes and begin your first quest.

Tip: Take a few minutes to adjust your hotkeys and UI settings for a smoother gameplay experience.

Classes & Multiclassing

THJ offers a unique multiclassing system where you can choose up to three classes:

  • First Class: Selected during character creation.
  • Second & Third Classes: Obtainable by speaking to the relevant Class Guild Masters in the Bazaar.

Key Points:

  • There are no "wrong" class combinations; creativity is encouraged!
  • You get one free class change. Additional changes can be purchased with Echoes of Memory (EoM).
  • Experiment with different class synergies to find what suits your playstyle.

Alternate Advancement (AA)

Alternate Advancement (AA) allows you to gain additional passive and active abilities.

How to Use AA Points:

  • Access the AA panel by pressing V (default key).
  • Use the plus (+) and minus (-) buttons to adjust the percentage of experience directed toward AAs.
  • Resets:
    • One free reset available per character via Ayonae in the Bazaar.
    • Additional resets require Echoes of Memory (EoM).

For an in-depth look at AAs, refer to the AA System Guide or join the community on Discord for advice.

Your First Quest

Steps to Begin:

  1. Speak to the Class Guild Masters in the Bazaar (refer to the map for exact locations).
  2. Select and confirm your second and third classes.
  3. Return to your starting location.
  4. Hand the item from your cursor to A Fading Memory NPC.
  5. Receive a starter bag and essential items to aid your journey.

Note: Merchants for spells and combat abilities are located near the guild masters.

Getting Around Norrath

Navigating the vast world of Norrath is made easier with the teleportation system provided by Tearel, located in the middle building of the Bazaar.

Using Tearel's Services:

  • Hail Tearel to attune yourself to various rune circles across Norrath.
  • Some locations are unlocked by default; others require discovery before use.
  • To teleport:
    1. Specify a location via the dialogue.
    2. Click the map next to Tearel.
    3. Confirm the teleport prompt to travel.

Tip: Regularly attune to new locations to expand your travel options.
