Welcome to the Zone Connection Finder

Looking for the fastest way to travel between zones? Use this tool to find the best path from your starting zone to your destination!

  • Select your starting zone in the From field.
  • Choose your destination in the To field.
  • Check or uncheck the Allow Bazaar Portal option to include or exclude special teleport routes.
  • Click Find Path to see the best way to get there!
