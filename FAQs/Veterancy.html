AA Veterancy Mechanism

Introduction

The AA Veterancy Mechanism rewards players with an AA bonus cap based on their existing characters' spent AAs. This system allows you to level up new characters' AAs more quickly while encouraging you to maintain multiple active characters.

Overview

The AA Veterancy system calculates a special AA bonus cap for your characters based on how many additional characters you have leveled. Only characters other than the one currently logged in are counted toward this total.

  • Subtract 150 from each character's spent AAs.
  • If the result is negative, treat it as 0.
  • Add all the positive results together to get the total bonus.
  • This total becomes your new AA bonus cap if it exceeds 150.

Detailed Calculation

Below is a step-by-step outline of how to calculate your AA Veterancy total:

  1. Check each alt characterâ€™s spent AAs.
  2. Apply (Spent AAs - 150) for each alt.
  3. If any result is below 0, count that as 0 instead.
  4. Add all resulting values to get your total potential AA bonus cap.
  5. If that sum is greater than 150, it overrides the default bonus cap.

Example

Consider the following three characters and their spent AAs:

  • Character A: 200 spent AAs
  • Character B: 300 spent AAs
  • Character C: 150 spent AAs

After applying the formula (Spent AAs - 150) (treat negatives as 0):

  • Character A: (200 - 150) = 50
  • Character B: (300 - 150) = 150
  • Character C: (150 - 150) = 0

Total = 50 + 150 + 0 = 200

Since 200 is greater than the default 150, your new AA bonus cap is 200.

Bonus Mechanic Explained

You receive a 300% uncapped bonus EXP for your AA Veterancy Bonus. However, this 300% bonus gradually scales down as you approach your maximum Veterancy (i.e., as you near the end of your Veteran AA).

Important Note: The currently logged-in character is not counted toward the total. Only additional characters (alts) contribute to your bonus cap.

Tips, Tricks, and Strategies

Level Multiple Characters: Having more alts with AAs above 150 helps push your total bonus higher.

Balance Your Playtime: Keep your alts progressing so their spent AAs increase over time.

Use the Bonus Wisely: Plan to grind AAs on new characters when your total Veterancy is high.
